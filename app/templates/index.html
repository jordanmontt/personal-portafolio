About Me

I am a software engineer that currently works as a research engineer at INRIA - Lille (National Institut or Research in Science and Technology, France, Lille).

Currently, I work on the Pharo AI project contributing and building different libraries for machine learning algorithms, statistical metrics, data preprocessing, and AI in general.
In INRIA, I also worked for 5 months as a Moose engineer mostly developing tools for Moose IDE project.

I have 2 years of experience developing open source projects and 1 year of experience working for web developing companies.
