About Me

I am a software engineer that currently works as a research engineer at INRIA - Lille (National Institut or Research in Science and Technology, France, Lille).

Currently, I started on working on Profilers for object oriented languages to later work on the Pharo Virtual Machine.

I was the main developer for one year of the Pharo AI project contributing and building different libraries for machine learning algorithms, statistical metrics, data preprocessing, and AI in general.
In INRIA, I also worked for 5 months as a Moose engineer mostly developing tools for Moose IDE project.

I work in different open source projects since 2020. I have 1 year of experience working for web developing companies.
